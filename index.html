The Problem
Free trials were growing, but conversion into qualified opportunities was flat. Onboarding was manual, product signals were buried across tools, and Sales had no clear view of which accounts were ready to talk.
The Solution
With AcmeCloud, the team unified product usage, lifecycle emails, and in-app journeys. PQL scores surfaced in real time, giving Sales a simple, actionable queue of high-intent accounts every day.
